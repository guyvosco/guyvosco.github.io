# About Lizzy

Lizzy is a six-year-old tabby cat born in Azor. She has a good-hearted yet fierce personality, and she loves sunny spots, soft blankets, and food (especially food). Her beautiful striped coat and gentle eyes make her a beloved companion to everyone she deems worthy and allows to get close.
